About Me

I am an Associate Software Engineer at Tech Mahindra with a strong foundation in Computer Science and a deep passion for Artificial Intelligence.

My primary interest lies in AI research, particularly mechanistic interpretability, where I aim to better understand how intelligent systems think and make decisions.

Driven by curiosity and a problem-solving mindset, I enjoy tackling complex challenges and continuously expanding my knowledge at the intersection of theory and real-world applications. I am committed to long-term growth in AI, with the goal of contributing meaningfully to cutting-edge research and impactful innovations.

Beyond building and learning, I have a natural inclination toward teaching and sharing knowledge. I find great fulfillment in breaking down complex ideas and helping others understand them, contributing to a more informed and collaborative tech community.

I am always open to meaningful collaborations, research opportunities, and discussions that push the boundaries of AI. Let's connect and build something impactful. 🚀
